Racquetball

This is a site for resources relating to the sport of racquetball. If you have something you'd like to add, submit an issue or even better, a pull request.

I have made several vector graphics for illustrations on this site, including a base court graphic. Feel free to download and modify it to make your own graphics. Unlike photos, vector graphics can be resized to any size without losing quality, so they are ideal for both websites and printing at different sizes.
